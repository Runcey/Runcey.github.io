Hobbies

Outside of work, you'll find me tinkering with my fully custom 3D printer, flying FPV drones, or taking photos of the world. My love for anything that flies led me to building my first fixed-wing drones when I was 7, and I was able to pursue that interest all the way to this day through clubs, personal projects, and other fun things! I still fly drones to this day, although I've switched to mainly multirotors, which has helped me get into aerial videography.
